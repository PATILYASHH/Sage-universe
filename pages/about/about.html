About Us

Welcome to Winter Sage Hackathon 2024-25! We are a community-driven organization dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and growth in the tech space. Our goal is to bring like-minded individuals together to solve real-world challenges through creative and technical solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for participants to showcase their skills, build networks, and contribute to open-source projects. We focus on promoting collaboration and creating opportunities for all.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a global community where everyone can contribute to making technology more accessible and impactful. We believe in empowering individuals and organizations to make a positive difference in the world.
